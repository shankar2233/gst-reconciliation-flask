About GST Reconciliation Tool

What is GST Reconciliation?

GST Reconciliation is the process of matching your purchase records (from Tally) with the supplier data available in GSTR-2A to ensure accuracy and compliance.

Key Features
  • AI-powered matching
  • Automatic discrepancy detection
  • Comprehensive reporting
  • Excel export functionality
  • Real-time processing
  • Secure data handling
How It Works
  1. Upload your Tally export and GSTR-2A data in Excel format
  2. Our AI engine processes and matches the records based on GSTIN and Invoice Number
  3. Identify matched records, discrepancies, and unmatched entries
  4. Download detailed reconciliation report for further analysis
